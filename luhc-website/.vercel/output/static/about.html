Our Club

We take pride in offering both competitive teams and a non-competitive development squad. Whoever you are, there is a place for you at LUHC.

Mission & Values

Inclusivity

We welcome players of all backgrounds and skill levels. From complete beginners to seasoned pros, we foster a supportive environment for everyone.

Excellence

On and off the pitch, we aim to excel. Through rigorous training, competitive spirit, and club development, we push for the win.

Community

Beyond hockey, we build lasting friendships. Our socials, charity events, and traditions create a family away from home.
