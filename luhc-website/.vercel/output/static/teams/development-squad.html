About The Squad

Whether you're picking up a stick for the first time or looking to sharpen your skills for competitive selection, Dev is your home.

Sessions are run by our dedicated Development Squad Coaches alongside the Dev Captains. It is the perfect pathway into the 3rd XI, or simply a great way to stay fit and make friends.
