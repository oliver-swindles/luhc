About LUMHC

We field three competitive teams in BUCS and Saturday leagues, offering a level for everyone. From elite performance in the 1st XI to the competitive spirit of the 3rd XI, we train hard and play harder.

Off the pitch, we are a brotherhood. Whether you play every week or join as a social member, you become part of a community that lasts a lifetime.
